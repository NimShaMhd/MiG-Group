MiG Groups

MiG is created from a wage idea from one of my dream to plant 1000 trees. From that dream, the MiG changed to MiG Groups and now we consist of 5 business companies and 3 Non-profit Organisations. The happy news is we are about to start our new firm MiG Fashions. The very first company we started is MiG Garden and from the profit savings, we start creating our dream project-MiG Forest. After that new ideas hit me. Agricultural sector is the riskiest sector but most important. So we planned to start MiG Farm. We recruited persons who have a great font for agriculture and we pay them a salary. This attracted more youngsters to the agricultural sector. The excess products thus created through MiG Farm is made available for everyone through creating MiG Stores. Our latest firm started are MiG Books and MiG MiniStore. These are for books, stationary things, and gifts. We are in the last preparation for our new venture MiG Fashions. Be ready to choose your outfits from us. Your support is our strength and it takes us to height and we are hoping it will last forever.
Live with nature, Change with nature, Grow with nature. Let the change to be a big step.

MiG Care

We are giving them proper education, health care facilities, skill development training and we are making them the best humans and independent. We have a dedicated group of doctors and trainers meant for them. We learn them a skill and help them to earn from their skills. All women in our family are working and earning their income.
