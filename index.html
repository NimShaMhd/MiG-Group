About

We started our journey in 2015 and since then we are expanding our group and now it includes 6 business firms and 2 non-profit organizations.We are making milestones in our businesses along with implementing the main motto of our group-"Make it Green". There are around 500 employees in our firms alltogether and around 300 members in our MiG Care family. The MiG Create make the members of our family independent and each one is able to save good amount of money through their skills. The products from our farmland are made available for you through our MiG Store.Not only MiG products but also you can get every household items there. We are extremly happy that the whole world is accepting us. The changes are not yet to the end. These are only a start.There are many on the way.
